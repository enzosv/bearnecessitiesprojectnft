Background

The Bear Necessities Project is a collection of 10,000 unique, collectible NFT bears living in the Ethereum blockchain. Each of these bears was heavily invested in the stock market and severely affected when everything came crashing down during the bear market. They all moved to the Ethereum blockchain and became NFTs in hopes that you will invest in them so they can all have a fresh start. Each bear is randomly generated from a set of individual traits, each conceptualized and hand-drawn over the course of a few months by our team’s artist, Peter Andrew Prado.

Our vision is to be able to grow a wonderful and fun community behind The Bear Necessities Project while also being able to give back to other communities, including the communities that these bears were originally from. With that being said, we would also like to mention that we will be reserving 15% of all proceeds for donations to charity and other foundations, with 10% going specifically to WWF, and 5% toward various other organizations.
